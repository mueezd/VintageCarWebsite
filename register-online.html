Registration Overview

The Vintage Car rally is divided into the 3 events.

  • Extraordinary cars from the Edwardian era (1908-1919) car show;
  • Vintage (pre-1931);
  • 1950s Sports Racing Cars

Spectators :

Spectators can enjoy this event by purchasing tickets online

Participants:

Interested participants/countries need to register online by their profile. The organizer will justify the participant profile and assess the participant.

The requirement of participation

  • The Car must-have vintage model.
  • Driver had the experience to participate similar type of vintage car rally.
  • The car condition must be suitable for participation.

General member:

General members can get event news/ news latter/ videos and other offers from the vintage car club.
