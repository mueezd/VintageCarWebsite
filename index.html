Rallies for Vintage & Classic Cars

Vintage rallying, often known as historic rallying, is a road rally-style that can be completed with most classic cars and requires no extra equipment (the equipment allowed depends on the particular rally). These rallies are more about fun than speed, and they can be a great way to get started in historic motorsport (which also include race meetings, classic endurance, and hill climbing). A classic road rally isn't about speed; in fact, finishing too early might result in harsh fines. The goal of a rally is to get from one point to another in a defined amount of time (time restrictions) , not too fast or too slow, and to try to equal the organizers' speed average.


Rallying for Vintage and Classic Cars has a new name...

We have a new name, but we're seasoned veterans. We've planned and delivered events on each of the five continents. We've logged thousands of kilometers of rallying throughout the world between us, and some of us have gone on to have successful careers in motorsport. Our passion has led us to establish Rally the Globe as a commercial but non-profit members' club dedicated to restoring our sport's original ethos.

Our club caters to a diverse group of motorsport fans that desire to travel the world in historic and classic vehicles. We organize competitive endurance category rallies with extensive off-road parts that take us throughout all of the world's continents. We organize competitive Marathon and Challenge category rallies with large off-road sections that will test participants, as well as Carrera category rallies that will travel across the world in a lighter competition environment with minimal off-road sections and at a more leisurely pace.
